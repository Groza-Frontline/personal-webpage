Room People Counter
Introduction to Web Design Course Subject
May 1, 2024

A project where hardware meets coding. This is a room people counter which senses and records the number of people entering a room. The counter was placed near a doorway connected to a laptop. I have programmed it so that when it reaches a certain amount of count, it will beep, indicating the room has accommodated the maximum number of people it can hold.
